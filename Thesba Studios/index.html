About THESBA

THESBA is a creative conglomerate founded by visionary artist and entrepreneur TELAI, with a bold mission to amplify voices and redefine the landscape of entertainment across music, media, and beyond.

More than just a label or a studio, THESBA is a movement—built to empower rising talents, tell untold stories, and create culturally resonant experiences that shape the future of African creativity.

From curated playlists to original content and disruptive campaigns, we're building a new wave—loud, intentional, and unapologetically different.
